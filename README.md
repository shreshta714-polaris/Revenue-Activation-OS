# Revenue Activation OS

Revenue Activation OS is an AI-powered Revenue Operations intelligence platform designed for Revenue Operations leaders, Sales Managers, Customer Success teams, and GTM organizations.

The platform transforms raw CRM pipeline data into actionable business insights by identifying revenue bottlenecks, pipeline inefficiencies, customer health risks, sales coaching opportunities, and growth recommendations.

Unlike traditional sales dashboards, Revenue Activation OS combines analytics, business intelligence, and an extensible multi-agent architecture to deliver executive-level recommendations that help teams accelerate revenue growth.

## Key Features

* Executive Revenue Dashboard
* Pipeline Health Monitoring
* Sales Performance Analytics
* Customer Health & Adoption Metrics
* High-Risk Opportunity Detection
* Pipeline Bottleneck Analysis
* Revenue Intelligence Reports
* AI-Ready Multi-Agent Architecture
* Interactive Streamlit Dashboard
* Business Recommendation Engine
